About Me

First and foremost, I love writing code. Ever since writing my first program in Visual Basic and manipulating it to solve a desired problem, I have cultivated a keen interest in the idea of solving real-world problems with software applications. Today, I employ my knowledge of Javascript to solve the never-ending puzzle of how to create a program that does exactly what I want it to do. I strongly believe in the power of programming to transform and improve the lives of people around the world--particularly when it comes to accessibility. Just as we think of accessibility as benefiting those with disabilities and the subsequent passing of laws affording equal rights to everyone--we must consider those with visual impairments when we design software applications. It is not right to exclude anyone from accessing a website. We are all different, but we are all human, and therefore have the same human rights. For my first professional application, I explored refactoring a website's code into something more modern, concise, and readable. My second assignment explored responsive, accessible website design using Bootstrap as a tool to increase mobile and tablet navigation and readability. I have developed fun quizzes to test your knowledge on a variety of subjects. For many years I have bug-tested and feature-tested various passion projects and software of multi-billion dollar companies and applications that I used passionately and ardently. Currently--I work in teams to develop various web apps while navigating version control and sequential processing. Even on my own time, I tend to continue writing code. Most recently I have worked on a website for my pickle-ball group's tournament play. I love staying engaged and competitive in a variety of activities--whether it's Scrabble in local tournaments or competing on the national stage in sailing competitions. I find a beautiful crossover in the pursuits that allow me to utilize my skills in software development while pursuing my interests in the things that I love: creativity, organization, problem solving, competitive-spirit, and an enjoyment for the natural world. This is what drives my passion--a never ending thirst to create beautiful, powerful things and share them with the world.
